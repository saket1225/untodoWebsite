Features

Built for people who
want the truth

Daily task tracking

Add tasks with priorities, categories, subtasks, and notes. Incomplete tasks carry over automatically. Long-press for quick actions. Simple, fast, no friction.

Living wallpaper

Your phone wallpaper becomes a dot grid that reflects your actual completion rate. Bright dots for good days, dark dots for missed ones. A countdown to your goal, updated daily.

Silicon AI partner

Connect your Silicon AI instance for remote task management, brutally honest weekly reviews, daily summaries, and nudge notifications.

Streaks & progress

Weekly completion charts, habit streaks, 14-day history. See exactly where you're falling short with data, not feelings.

Pomodoro timer

Six science-backed presets from 15-minute sprints to 90-minute deep blocks. Fullscreen immersive mode with per-task time tracking.
